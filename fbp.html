Faith Based Pilot

Glass-deck media hub — messages on faith and flight, without the noise.

FaithBasedPilot.net is the official watching and listening site for Faith Based Pilot — a focused place to experience messages on faith, and flight without distractions or algorithm clutter. Everything FBP publishes in video or audio form lives here — simple, clear, and direct.

Why this .net site exists

The .net site is a dedicated media hub — built to watch and listen. The FaithBasedPilot.org site focuses on written studies, resources, and long-form material. Splitting the two keeps both fast and easy to navigate.

Quick guide:
  • .net → watching & listening (videos, series)
  • .org → reading & research (articles, resources)

YouTube & Rumble

Faith Based Pilot publishes on both YouTube and Rumble. YouTube is the main platform for reach. Rumble is a secure backup — so every video stays available even if YouTube ever restricts content.

Mission

Faith Based Pilot exists to encourage strength, discipline, and truth in everyday life — in the gym, in the cockpit, and in the daily walk of faith. Each message aims to challenge the mind, build conviction, and show what living boldly in faith looks like.
