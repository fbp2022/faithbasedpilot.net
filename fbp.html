Faith Based Pilot (.net)

Watch. Listen. Strengthen your faith.

FaithBasedPilot.net is the official watching and listening site for Faith Based Pilot — a focused place to experience messages on faith, fitness, and flight without distractions or algorithm noise. This is where everything FBP publishes in video or audio form lives together — simple, clear, and direct.

Why This .NET Site Exists

The .net site was created as a dedicated media hub — a place to watch and listen. Meanwhile, the FaithBasedPilot.org site focuses on written studies, resources, and long-form material. Separating the two makes both simpler and faster to navigate.

Quick guide:
  • .net → watching & listening hub (videos, audio, series)
  • .org → reading & research (articles, references, resources)

YouTube & Rumble

Faith Based Pilot publishes on both YouTube and Rumble. YouTube serves as the main platform for reach and visibility. Rumble exists as a secure backup — ensuring every video remains available even if YouTube ever restricts content. This dual setup keeps the message online, uncensored, and always accessible.

Mission

Faith Based Pilot exists to encourage strength, discipline, and truth in everyday life — in the gym, in the cockpit, and in the daily walk of faith. Each message aims to challenge the mind, build conviction, and show what living boldly in faith really looks like.
