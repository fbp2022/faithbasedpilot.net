Welcome to The Hangar

FaithBasedPilot.net was created to serve as the home for all flight-related content and personal projects tied to aviation. Unlike FaithBasedPilot.org, which focuses on ministry, devotions, and faith-based writing, this site is designed to showcase videos, podcasts, and creative media centered around flying, training, and the love of aviation.

This separation allows each site to serve its unique purpose without overlap: the .org remains focused on faith and study, while the .net provides a dedicated space for aviation adventures, personal reflections, and additional content like piano performances.

Thank you for visiting. Use the navigation above to explore flight videos or enjoy some bonus piano content.
